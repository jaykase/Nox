Admission and Enrollment

Basic Admission Requirements

Incoming Freshmen.

Prior submission of the following document before enrollment

  • Form 138 (High School Card)
  • Birth Certificate
  • Good Moral Character Certificate

Foreign Students

Prior submission of the following document before enrollment

  • Authenticated Transcript of Records
  • National Intelligence Coordinating Agency (NICA) Clearance
  • Quarantine Certificate
  • Notice of Acceptance (NOA)
  • Notarized Bureau of Immigration Form
  • Notarized Authorization for processing of Student Visa by the School

LAW Students

Prior submission of the following document before enrollment

  • Authenticated Transcript of Records
  • Exam Results
